?? COMMON TASKS ???????????????????????????????????????????????????????????????
?                                                                             ?
? TASK: Open a Website                                                       ?
? STEPS:                                                                     ?
?   1. Click on Address Bar                                                 ?
?   2. Type website URL or search term                                      ?
?   3. Press Enter or click Go button                                       ?
?                                                                             ?
? TASK: Download a File                                                     ?
? STEPS:                                                                     ?
?   1. Click on download link on website                                    ?
?   2. Download Manager window opens                                        ?
?   3. Monitor progress in real-time                                        ?
?   4. File saved to Downloads folder when complete                        ?
?                                                                             ?
? TASK: Open Downloaded Files                                              ?
? STEPS:                                                                     ?
?   1. In Download Manager, click "Open Folder"                            ?
?   2. Windows Explorer opens showing Downloads folder                      ?
?   3. Double-click file to open                                            ?
?                                                                             ?
? TASK: Pause a Download                                                   ?
? STEPS:                                                                     ?
?   1. Click on download in list to select                                  ?
?   2. Click "Pause" button                                                 ?
?   3. Download pauses immediately                                           ?
?   4. Click "Resume" to continue                                           ?
?                                                                             ?
? TASK: Change Search Engine                                                ?
? STEPS:                                                                     ?
?   1. Click dropdown next to search bar                                    ?
?   2. Select Google, Bing, Yahoo, or DuckDuckGo                           ?
?   3. Future searches use selected engine                                  ?
?                                                                             ?

?? TROUBLESHOOTING ????????????????????????????????????????????????????????????
?                                                                             ?
? Problem: Browser won't start                                               ?
? Solution: Ensure .NET Framework 4.7.2 is installed                        ?
?                                                                             ?
? Problem: Download doesn't start                                            ?
? Solution: Check internet connection, verify URL is correct               ?
?                                                                             ?
? Problem: Download very slow                                                ?
? Solution: Check internet speed, try pausing other downloads               ?
?                                                                             ?
? Problem: File not found after download                                    ?
? Solution: Click "Open Folder" to check Downloads directory               ?
?                                                                             ?
? Problem: "File already exists" message                                    ?
? Solution: File will be saved with (1), (2), etc. appended to name        ?
?                                                                             ?
? Problem: Download keeps retrying                                          ?
? Solution: Server may be slow/down. Try different file or wait            ?
?                                                                             ?
